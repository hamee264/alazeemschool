Who We Are

Al-Azeem Model College is a dynamic institution in Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State, committed to academic excellence, innovation, and discipline. With a legacy of shaping future leaders, we welcome you to be part of our success story.

🏅 Academic Excellence

Consistent record of top performances in WAEC and NECO examinations.

📚 Moral Discipline

We instill sound moral values and discipline in all our students.

🏫 Modern Facilities

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art classrooms and laboratories.

🎯 Vision

To build ideal and Islamic spirited educated individuals, who are outstanding role models and exemplary citizens in the diverse society.

🕌 Mission

Creating learning environment that produces active and excellent students, through the instrumentality of Islamic oriented knowledge, values and inspiration.

Transmitting worthwhile secular and Islamic knowledge, providing safe upbringing away from negative influences, and developing strong character through core values of Islam.

Maintaining well certified, god-fearing, zealous and highly productive staffs, who integrate ideal Islamic principles, ethics, morals and self disciplines, towards excellent achievement of goals of the school.
